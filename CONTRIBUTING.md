# Contributing

This is a commons, not a project. There are no maintainers, no roadmap, no deliverables.

If you find something here worth picking up — to formalize, test, extend, or disprove — please do. That's the point.

---

## What would actually be useful

**Formalizing the framework:**
- Dynamical systems analysis of the attractor geometry claims
- Information-theoretic grounding of "compatibility bandwidth"
- Formal definition of the relay ↔ diffusion regime boundary

**Empirical work:**
- Probing Hyperloop's internal representations across loop iterations (do they form coherent trajectories?)
- Toy model implementation of the two-regime propagation model
- Testing whether loop depth correlates with diffusion regime emergence

**Connecting to existing literature:**
- Biological analogues — does the pattern lifecycle map onto anything in neural development or evolutionary biology?
- Relationship to existing work on attractor networks, reservoir computing, or dynamical systems approaches to cognition
- Connection to evolvability literature (Nugget 8 suggests this)

**Critique:**
- Identifying where the framework makes predictions that could be falsified
- Finding where "theory of how intelligence works" and "theory of how we'd want it to work" diverge

---

## How to contribute

Open an issue or PR. No formal process. If you're building on this, a note in your own work pointing back here would be appreciated but is not required.
